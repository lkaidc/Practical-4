About LeafSpace

LeafSpace is an online shopping platform for digital and physical copies of books! Similar to other online book stores, this is mainly located in the Philippines featuring Filipino and international books.

Vision

To become the leading online destination for readers to go to by making books, references, digital stories, and online resources accessible, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone.

Mission

Our mission is to connect and provide readers with diverse resources, fictional, non-fictional, physical, and digital books through a convenient, comfortable, and user-friendly platform. We are committed to showcasing local works and ensuring that Filipino stories are recognized, celebrated, and accessible, fostering pride for our culture and community alongside other global literature.
