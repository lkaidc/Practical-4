Call Me By Your Name

Author: André Aciman

Genre: Romance, Fiction, LGBT

Description

Call Me by Your Name follows the unforgettable summer romance between Elio, a teenage boy, and Oliver, a visiting scholar on the Italian Riviera. As their feelings grow, they experience love, self-discovery, and a deep connection that leaves a lasting impact on both of their lives.
