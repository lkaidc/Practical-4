The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo

Author: Taylor Jenkins Reid

Genre: Historical Fiction, Romance

Description

Aging Hollywood icon Evelyn Hugo is finally ready to tell the truth about her glamorous and scandalous life. She shares the story of her rise to fame, her seven marriages, and the sacrifices she made for love, ambition, and success with an unknown magazine reporter, Monique Grant.
